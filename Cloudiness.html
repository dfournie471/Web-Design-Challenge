Cloudiness

Comparing cloudiness to latitude position is tricky. There are many variables that determine cloud cover of a city on any given day. One of the most important variables is proximity to large bodies of water which this data set does not take into account. However, looking at the data we see that for 01/05/17 northern hemisphere cities tended to have more 0% cloud cover days, and more higher percentage cloud cover days, while southern hemisphere cities tended to be more spread out %-wise.
