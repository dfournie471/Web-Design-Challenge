Max Temperature

As expected, city temperatures measure warmer the closer the city is to the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). Because measurements were taken in January, it is reasonable to expect that southern hemisphere cities measured higher temperatures due to the tilt of the earth. (i.e it is winter for the northern hemisphere and summer for the southern hemisphere).
